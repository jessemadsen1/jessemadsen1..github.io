OUR STORY

Tana wanted to practice Italian before visiting her kids studying in Rome, so she downloaded a conversation app where she met Pietro. When she mentioned she was headed to Rome shortly, Pietro said that’s where he lived and that they should go grab a cup of coffee.

~ The rest is history ~
